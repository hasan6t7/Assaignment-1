Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Sustainability

This pillar emphasizes environmental protection, climate change adaptation, and sustainable resource management to ensure a healthy and livable planet for future generations.

Good Governance

This pillar promotes transparency, accountability, and the rule of law to ensure a fair and just society for all Bangladeshis.

Digital Bangladesh

This pillar seeks to leverage technology to improve governance, education, healthcare, and other sectors, making Bangladesh a more connected and efficient nation.

Human Capital Development

This pillar focuses on investing in education, healthcare, and social safety nets to improve the quality of life and productivity of the Bangladeshi people.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
